# Google Data Analytics – Bellabeat Case Study

This project analyzes smart‑device usage data to generate insights for Bellabeat, a wellness technology company. The goal is to understand how users engage with their devices and how Bellabeat can strengthen its marketing strategy.

---

## Objectives

- Explore daily activity, sleep, and step‑count patterns  
- Identify trends in user engagement  
- Connect behavioural patterns to potential marketing opportunities  
- Produce clear, actionable recommendations  

---

## Key Findings

- Users with consistent sleep patterns tend to maintain higher activity levels  
- Step counts and active minutes vary strongly by weekday  
- Many users under‑utilize advanced device features  
- Engagement increases when users track multiple metrics together  

---

## Deliverables

- Cleaned and structured Fitbit‑style activity data  
- Visualizations of daily, weekly, and monthly behaviour  
- A concise set of marketing recommendations  
- A final summary report  

---

## Tools Used

- R / RStudio  
- tidyverse  
- ggplot2  
- spreadsheets for initial review  

---

This case study demonstrates practical data‑analysis skills and the ability to translate behavioural data into clear business recommendations.
